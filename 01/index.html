Spaghetti: the most typical italian dish

Loved everywhere around the world, Spaghetti are a pasta shape typical of italian cuisine.

Spaghetti picture from Wikipedia

Recipe and serving suggestions follow.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Fresh Tomatoes
  • Fresh Basil
  • Garlic
  • Onions
  • Pepper
  • Parmigiano Cheese

Method

  1. Let a pot of water boil and add a pinch of salts
  2. Add pasta
  3. Let fresh ingredients simmer in a pan
  4. Drain pasta after 10 minutes
  5. Add sauce
  6. Serve warm with some Parmigiano cheese sprinkled on top
